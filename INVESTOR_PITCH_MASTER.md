## ‚ùì INVESTOR Q&A (The Deep Dive)

### **Q: How do you prevent users from going off-platform?**
**A:** We use a "Retention Sandwich." On top, we provide **Trust** (Escrow & Disputes) that users can't get offline. On the bottom, we provide **Incentives** (XP, Streaks, and Level-up perks). If you go off-platform, you lose your Trust Score, your Rewards, and your Security.

### **Q: Who are your main competitors?**
**A:** We compete with **Legacy Marketplaces** (Fiverr/Upwork) and **Informal Networks** (WhatsApp Groups). Compared to Legacy, we are 10x faster and better for mobile. Compared to WhatsApp, we provide 10x more security and structure.

### **Q: What is the biggest technical risk?**
**A:** Real-time scaling. We‚Äôve mitigated this by choosing **Supabase & Postgres**, allowing for massive horizontal scale without the DevOps overhead of a custom backend.

### **Q: How do you handle fake profiles and scams?**
