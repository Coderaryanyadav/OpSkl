### 1. AI Mission Architect (The Brain)
*   **Natural Language Deployment**: Clients can describe a project in plain English or Hindi. Our integrated AI (Gemini) automatically parses the requirements into structured payloads, defining budgets, categories, and technical milestones.
*   **Intelligent Matching**: The AI cross-references mission requirements against the talent pool's clearance level and verified skills to ensure high-fidelity pairings.

### 2. The Talent Experience (Cyber-Premium)
*   **Swipe-to-Apply**: A high-fidelity, haptic-synchronized gesture system for rapid mission deployment.
*   **Clearance Levels**: A gamified reputation system (XP-based). Higher reputation unlocks access to "Level 1" and "Level 2" elite missions with higher budget caps.
*   **Portfolio Vault**: A secure, encrypted indexing system for professional assets, allowing talents to showcase their work with verifiable proof.

### 3. Trust & Safety Protocol (The Shield)
*   **Aadhaar e-KYC Bridge**: Every talent on the platform passes through a server-authoritative identity gate, verified against government records.
*   **UPI Native Escrow**: Funds are locked digitally via the OpSkl Financial Circuit (Razorpay) before a mission begins, ensuring talents are paid and clients are protected.
*   **Global SOS Signal**: A field-ready safety node integrated into the talent feed. One tap triggers a location-pinned distress frequency to local emergency responders.

### 4. Geo-Spatial Discovery (The Radar)
*   **Radius Filtering**: Precision mission discovery using PostGIS. Talents only see opportunities within a 20km urban perimeter (or customized radius), reducing commute friction and increasing real-world efficiency.
